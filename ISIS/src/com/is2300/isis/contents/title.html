Integrity Solutions Make Installer

This Wizard will guide you through creating an installer for your project. Follow the steps to provide all of the information that is needed in order for your installer wizard to be generated.

Navigating the Wizard

Navigate through this Wizard by clicking on the Next and Back buttons, as needed. You will only be able to move to the next step once all required information in the current step is provided. Until then, the Next button will be disabled.

Completing the Wizard

Once you have provided all required information, you will be taken to a Summary page. On this page, you can review the information you provided. If you need to make changes, simply click the Back button to return to the step where the change(s) needs to be made and make the change. Once everything is set up the way you would like it, click on the Finish button to complete the Wizard and generate your Installer Wizard. This task may take some time to complete, as your Installer Wizard will be compiled from generated source code files and JARchived into an executable JAR file for deployment. You can monitor the progress via the progress indicator on the Completing Task page. The more complex your desired Installation Wizard, the longer the generation task will take, so, please be patient.
